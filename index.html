How to Find the IMDB ID

The IMDB ID is a unique identifier for movies on IMDB. It usually starts with 'tt' followed by a series of numbers. For example, the IMDB ID for the movie 'Inception' is 'tt1375666'.

To find the IMDB ID, visit the IMDB website and search for the movie. Look at the URL of the movie's page; it will look something like this: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1234567/. The part after '/title/' and before the next '/' is the IMDB ID you need.

The DPWV saves your DPWV video in the url above, so you can share movies you like!
